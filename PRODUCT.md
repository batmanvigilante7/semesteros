# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

adaptive

## Users
General college students managing semesters. Their context is balancing multiple courses, lecture topics, deadlines, and study notes with minimal distraction and maximum efficiency.

## Product Purpose
Transform the academic workspace into a cohesive Academic Operating System (AOS) that helps students stay on track, balance workloads, and prioritize their studies.

## Positioning
A unified Academic Operating System designed with Notion-like simplicity and Linear-like performance.

## Brand Personality
Elegant, minimal, calm, premium, professional, and student-friendly. A design language heavily inspired by Apple, Linear, and Notion.

## Anti-references
- Avoid neon-drenched cyberpunk dashboard grids.
- Avoid over-decorated, crowded card walls.
- Avoid decorative uppercase eyebrows on every section.
- Avoid side-stripe borders as color accents.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity Over Decoration**: Clean typography, high-contrast layouts, and generous negative space guide focus.
- **Performance as a Feature**: Instant interactions, subtle micro-animations, and structured page layouts.
- **Intentional Spacing**: Strict adherence to an 8-point grid ensures rhythmic consistency.
- **Adaptive Semantics**: Design variables and components scale naturally across viewports.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- AAA contrast compliance for typography (ink-on-white).
- Strict keyboard focus rings and full ARIA semantics on all interactive elements.
- Reduced motion overrides (`prefers-reduced-motion`) for all Framer Motion animations.
